World cup '98 was my first big football memory. I was eight at the time, the same age Julián "claims" to be in *Despelote*. Fragments of my own past are coloured in by this memory and this phenomena is rendered on screen here in an uncanny way that I haven't experienced before. For many kids, football is the fabric of life that comforts us, proves to be a reliable constant through the trials and tribulations of growing up. For a two-three year period between the age of 12-15, I played football pretty much every day with my mates over the cricket field. Whatever else was going on, it was a vital sanctuary of sorts. This is a game about having a kick about and growing into your skin, and the ultimately hazy recollections of place that you will carry with you. 

Oh yeah, it's really short. I did it in two sittings but could've easily downed it in one.

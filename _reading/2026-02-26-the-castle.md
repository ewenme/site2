ngl I gave up about a third of the way in. I know that the book is meant to be a difficult read, that the struggle is in keeping with the mind-numbing bureaucracy K is trying to navigate, but I just wasn't feeling it at all. 

When I'm sat "consuming" I sometimes wonder how many more books/films/albums I will listen to before I die. When I was younger this number was unfathomably big but now it's small enough to imagine. A side effect of this phenomenon is that I have much less patience with stuff that isn't hitting. Even more so when it's a "classic" that I already have a rough sketch of intentions for. I'll give another one of his a go soon enough.

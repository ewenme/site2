Maybe a little bit on the nose in parts but ultimately an unflinching, sincere and hopeful slice of British film that we desperately need more of. Ken Loach is a singular filmmaker when it comes to examining working class communities and class struggle and I am so grateful for his life's work. Andy Dawson made a surprise cameo and I did the *Leo pointing at TV* thing. Ebla Mari (Yara) is wickec.

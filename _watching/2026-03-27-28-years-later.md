Pretty fun and generally interesting things are happening on the screen. Renders a poignant and affirming vision of humans making new myths and remembering the dead in spite of society shattering events. Petty and shitty interpersonal behaviour will go on. Honour and love will persist in even greater measure.

There's a silly, tongue-in-cheek energy that I didn't really mind but is a clear departure from the straight face of previous entries. It's a zombie flick, at the end of the day.
